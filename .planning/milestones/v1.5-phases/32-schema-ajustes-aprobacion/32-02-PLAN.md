           - `log_type`: z.enum(['IN', 'OUT']) — required
           - `justification`: z.string().min(10) — required
         - Branch EDIT:
           - `type`: literal `'EDIT'`
           - `employee_id`: z.number().int().positive() — required
           - `clock_log_id`: z.number().int().positive() — required (must reference existing log)
           - `new_timestamp`: z.string().datetime() — required (the corrected timestamp)
           - `log_type`: z.enum(['IN', 'OUT']) — required
           - `justification`: z.string().min(10) — required
         - Branch VOID:
           - `type`: literal `'VOID'`
           - `employee_id`: z.number().int().positive() — required
           - `clock_log_id`: z.number().int().positive() — required (the log to void)
           - `log_type`: z.enum(['IN', 'OUT']) — required
           - `justification`: z.string().min(10) — required
           - `new_timestamp`: omitted (not applicable for VOID)
